Singles Club was started in 2014 by two old friends, Chris Muccioli and Jeffrey Silverstein as an attempt to bridge the gap between the physical and digital music landscapes. We seek to tell the incredible stories of musicians and artists we’ve handpicked for each release via limited-run 7” records paired with long form features, photo essays, and more on our digital journal. We are writers, musicians, designers, and die-hard music fans.

Photo: Daniel Dorsa

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions we get. Don’t see something here and want to get in touch? Drop us a line to hello@singlesclub.fm and we’ll get back to you in a jiffy.

How do you select your artists?

Simply put, we’re looking for artists who are not only making great music, but those with stories to share. We listen to a TON of different music and are always looking to be inspired. Got someone you think we should hear? Let us know! Keep up with what we’re listening to on our soundcloud.

How much does a subscription to Singles Club cost?

Subscriptions are available for $55 + shipping. Single issues are available for $8 plus shipping.

What’s included in a standard subscription?

Lots! Head to our subscription page to learn more about what you can expect when you subscribe.

Can I buy an individual issue?

You bet! Individual issues are on sale for each release for $8 + shipping. Head here to learn more about what you’ll get with a single issue purchase.

Can I buy individual issues from Volume One?

Yes you can! Singles from Year 1 are available for $6 each right here.

How long does my subscription last?

A subscription to Singles Club will last you through four issues, January - January.

I changed my address and need to update my shipping info. Where can I do that?

No problem! Jump over to our change of address form and let us know that you've made a move.

Do you ship internationally?

You bet. Special international shipping rates apply.

I never received a shipping/confirmation email from you. What gives?

Each order should receive individual confirmation and shipping emails. If for some reason you haven’t received one, just let us know at hello@singlesclub.fm.

I’d like to submit my band for a potential Singles Club release. Where do I send music to?

Please send any/all demo submissions to hello@singlesclub.fm. We will do our best to listen to anything and everything you send us and try to get back to you in a speedy fashion.

I’m a writer, photographer, or videographer and want to work with you. How can I get in touch?

We are ALWAYS looking for more contributors. Drop us a line at hello@singlesclub.fm with a link to your portfolio, clips, etc. and we’ll be in touch.

I’d like have Singles Club carry our product in your shop. Can I make this happen?

We’re always open to ideas. Artwork, photography, you name it. Say hello at you guessed it, hello@singlesclub.fm and we’ll see what we can do.
