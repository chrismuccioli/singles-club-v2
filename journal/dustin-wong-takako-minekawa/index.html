Issue #008 | COMING SOON

Dustin Wong & Takako Minekawa

After a 13-year hiatus from Japanese shibuya-kei artist Takako Minekawa, a series of performances from Ponytail guitarist Dustin Wong would reinvigorate her. Soon after a few correspondences, 2013’s Toropical Circle marked their first collaborative effort, a playful and buoyant record that saw the onset of a lasting musical affinity. The follow-up LP, Savage Imagination found the two in symbiosis, Wong’s technical, loop-heavy guitar playing floating seamlessly in and out of the hushed and serene vocals of Minekawa. 

Prior to embarking on their first ever tour in China earlier this year, Takako and Dustin sent us ‘Payapaya’, an equally transcendental continuation of their already impressive catalogue. Issue 8 feat. ‘Payapaya’ is available for pre-order now exclusively in the shop via single issue purchase or subscription. Limited to 250 copies. 100 on clear wax for subscribers.
