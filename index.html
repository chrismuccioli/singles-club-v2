Small Sur

Small Sur is a Baltimore-bred folk-peace group spearheaded by songwriter Bob Keal. Keal, who left the South Dakota farm he was raised on at 14, spent time in California before landing in Maryland. Backed by a rotating cast of talented multi-instrumentalists, Keal’s soothing vocals are met with minimalist arrangements allowing the listener to simply ‘be’.
